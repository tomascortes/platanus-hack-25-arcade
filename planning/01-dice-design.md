# Dice Design Specification

## Visual Style

- **Retro aesthetic**: Clean, pixel-perfect dice with bold outlines
- **Neat appearance**: Sharp edges, clear dot patterns
- **Color scheme**: White dice with black dots, or colored dice variants
- **3D effect**: Subtle shadows and highlights for depth

## Dice Structure

- **Size**: 80x80 pixels (adjustable)
- **Shape**: Square with rounded corners (optional)
- **Faces**: 6 faces (1-6 dots)
- **Border**: 2-3px black outline

## Dot Patterns (Standard Dice Layout)

- **1**: Center dot
- **2**: Diagonal (top-left, bottom-right)
- **3**: Diagonal line (top-left, center, bottom-right)
- **4**: Four corners
- **5**: Four corners + center
- **6**: Two columns of three dots

## Dot Specifications

- **Dot size**: 8-10px radius
- **Dot color**: Black (or contrasting color)
- **Dot spacing**: Evenly distributed based on face value
- **Dot style**: Filled circles
